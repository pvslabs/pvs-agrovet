About Us

About Us

P.V.S. Agro Vet is a certified manufacturer and exporter of high-quality human, veterinary, poultry, aqua pharmaceuticals, and feed supplements. Located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India, we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety in all our products.

With certification in Current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP) by Transpacific Certifications Limited, we ensure strict adherence to internationally recognized manufacturing and management systems. Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver reliable and effective solutions that meet the evolving needs of the healthcare and animal nutrition industries.

At P.V.S. Agro Vet, we believe in quality, trust, and continuous improvement, ensuring that every product we manufacture upholds the best industry practices for safety and efficacy.

Discover a complete range of veterinary products designed to support the health and well-being of poultry, cattle, equines, and companion animals.

From nutritional supplements to health care solutions, our products are formulated to enhance growth, improve immunity, and ensure optimal productivity. Trust us for quality-driven care for all your livestock and pets.
